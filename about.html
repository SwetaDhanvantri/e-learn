Learn With Us

We give a great opportunity to those who does not want to travel and also those who want to study further but for some reason they do not have enough time to study. We facilitate you to study according your suitable time & according to your suitable place.

Earn With Us

“Earn with us” offer instructors a chance to make their career with Vidhyamani. Those who wants to share their skills with the learners, can apply at vidhyamani, they have to only share their resume & a short teaching video on our mail mansi@vidhyamani.com.

ABOUT US

Meaning of “Vidhyamani”

The word “Vidhyamani” is the combination of two words “Vidhya” (knowledge) and “mani” (precious stone) i.e. treasure of knowledge.


Vidhyamani logo represents

In Vidhyamani logo a bird eagle defines determination & focus; Just like an eagle, we want learners occupy full wisdom and explore their sky of knowledge.

How Vidhyamani works

Vidhyamani is basically a digital platform which plays a role of connector between trainee & educator. We provide smart education to children for their bright future.

Aim of Vidhyamani

Our aim is to give the right education in the right way so that the child enjoys the study and not considers it as an indulgence.
