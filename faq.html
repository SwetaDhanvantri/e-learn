FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How is Vidhyamani different from others?
Vidhyamani helps students to learn things with practical knowledge. Teachers teach the things in enjoyable manner so that child can learn easily. A special doubt class is conduct for students where they can solve theirs doubts.
Who is eligible to apply for Vidhyamani?
Vidhyamani provides academic classes for Class 9Th to 12Th. We also provide Skill based courses and special preparation for competitive exams.
How can anyone apply at Vidhyamani?
You can download Vidhyamani app from play store and register yourself for the course you want.
How can we join Vidhyamani as a teacher?
Vidhyamani provides vacancy from time to time, and then you can apply for the eligible post.
Does Vidhyamani cost too high for the courses?
No not at all. We cost nominal charges for the courses.
